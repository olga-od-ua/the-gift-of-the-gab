Regular-paced Learning

You can choose how often your lessons will occur from as few as one lesson a week if you choose individual lessons package. Our recommendation is at least two lessons a week with two to five hours of self-learning outside the classes. Our regular-paced groups attend two lessons a week.

When to choose it:
  • You are not in a rush
  • You have at least three months before landing in Ireland
  • You want a lot of time for self-learning
